Groundwater & Isotope Hydrology

Home Publications Education Projects Gallery Research

My research focuses on understanding the complex interactions between climate change, groundwater systems, and hydrological processes through the lens of environmental isotopes.

  • Isotope Hydrology: Using stable isotopes (δ18O, δ2H) to trace groundwater recharge sources and residence time.
  • Groundwater Sustainability: Assessing recharge dynamics and aquifer vulnerability under changing climate scenarios.
  • Climate Impact Studies: Investigating the long-term impact of precipitation variability and temperature rise on groundwater levels.
  • Modeling Approaches: Combining geostatistical models and isotope data to improve water resource predictions.
